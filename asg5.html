Poha Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flattened rice (poha) – 2 cups
  • Onion – 1 (chopped)
    • Alternative: Spring onion
  • Green chili – 2
  • Curry leaves – 8 to 10
  • Mustard seeds – 1 tsp
  • Potato – 1 (optional)
  • Lemon juice – 1 tbsp
  • Salt – as per taste

Steps

  1. Wash poha and keep it aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, onion, and green chili.
  3. Add potato cubes (if using) and cook until soft.
  4. Add poha and salt, mix gently.
  5. Switch off flame and add lemon juice before serving.
