Ahmedabad Ahmedabad is the largest city in the Indian state of Gujarat and a historic UNESCO World Heritage City known for its rich cultural heritage and multicultural architecture, including the Sultanate-period monuments, gated residential streets called pols, and domestic wooden architecture.
